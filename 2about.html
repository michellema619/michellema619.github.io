Taipei, Taiwan

I am full Taiwanese!

Born and raised in Taipei for the first nine years of my life, Taiwan is where I call home! Even though my time there was short, I was fully immersed in the culture.

Ever since I moved, the thing I missed the most, other than my family, is the food. From night market street foods to other traditional dishes, Taiwan is the go-to place if you want good food without breaking the bank.

Shanghai, China

When I was nine, my family moved to Shanghai. This was the first big change in my life. Not only did I have to start over in a new country, but transitioning to an all-English school and environment was even harder.

Although it was challenging, it helped me grow as a person. Within the international schools I attended, I met people from all over the world and developed a global perspective that now sets me apart from others.

Los Angeles, California

For college, I moved across the world to sunny California for USC. Going into my fourth year, I'm still loving the warm weather, diverse culture and endless advertures every single day.

Can't wait to see where the next chapter of my life will take me!
